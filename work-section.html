Work pathway

Move to Germany for skilled work

Germany actively recruits from Africa. New migration laws make it easier than ever for qualified professionals to secure work visas.

01

How the work visa works

  • Secure a job offer from a German employer
  • Employer begins sponsorship process
  • You apply for work visa with skill recognition documents
  • Visa typically issued within 4–8 weeks
02

What you need

  • Relevant qualifications or certifications
  • 1–3 years professional experience (typical)
  • Language skills (B1 German or job-specific English)
  • Valid passport and health insurance
03

Salary expectations

  • Entry-level roles: €20k–€28k/year
  • Mid-level (3–5 yrs): €28k–€45k/year
  • Senior roles: €45k–€70k+/year
  • Healthcare & IT typically pay above average
04

Timeline

  • Job search: 2–6 months
  • Visa sponsorship: 1–2 months
  • Visa processing: 4–8 weeks
  • Total: 4–9 months (typical)
