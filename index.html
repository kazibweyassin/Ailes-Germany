In-demand careers

Skills Germany is actively looking for

If your background matches one of these sectors, your Germany plan may be more realistic than you think.

Nursing

Healthcare staffing is a priority

Germany needs qualified nurses and caregivers across hospitals, elder care, and clinics.

IT

Digital roles stay in demand

Software, support, data, and systems roles remain important as German companies scale digitally.

Construction

Infrastructure keeps growing

Skilled trades, site support, and construction workers help fill shortages in large projects.

Logistics

Caregiving

Hospitality

Service sectors still hire
